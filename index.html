Your Qur'an Companion
Understand the Qur'an more deeply by discovering how ayat connect to one another — through shared meaning, shared Arabic root words, and authentic Hadith. Choose a translation in 2 English and 4 Urdu renditions, or let the What Am I Feeling? button guide you to verses by topic.
What it does
  • Search any English concept, Arabic word, or Ayah ID to find related verses.
  • See meaning-based pairs (semantic similarity) and root-word pairs (shared Arabic roots) side by side.
  • Each Qur'an ayah is paired with authentic Hadith on the same theme.
  • Tap Words & Roots to explore every root and token in an ayah.
  • Explore What Am I Feeling? to browse 58 curated topics and jump straight to relevant ayat.
  • Switch between 6 translations — Sahih International, Yusuf Ali, and 4 Urdu scholars — any time.
How to use
  1. Select a translation from the dropdown in the header.
  2. Choose English, Arabic, or Ayah ID search mode.
  3. Type your query and press Search or Enter.
  4. Click any result ayah to open its Pairs panel.
  5. Click a paired ayah or Hadith to dive deeper.
  6. Or tap What Am I Feeling? to browse by topic directly.
Connections are algorithm-generated to aid study. Always verify with trusted tafseer and scholars.
