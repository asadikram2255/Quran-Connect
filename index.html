Why this exists
Many people want to understand the Qur'an more deeply, but it's hard to connect related meanings across different ayahs and authentic ahadiths. This tool helps you explore those connections — through meaning, shared root words, and Hadith context — all in one place.
What it does
  • Find ayat related to a topic (English or Arabic keyword).
  • See meaning-based and root word connections side-by-side.
  • Dive deeper — click any paired ayah to explore its own connections.
How to use
  1. Pick input type: English, Arabic, or Ayah ID.
  2. Type your query and press Search (or Enter).
  3. Click an ayah in results to load its pairs.
  4. Click any paired ayah to keep exploring deeper.
Results are suggested links to aid study. Always verify with trusted tafseer and scholars.
