Why this exists
Many people want to understand the Qur’an more deeply, but it’s hard to connect related meanings across different ayahs and authentic ahadiths. This website helps you explore those connections quickly, in one place.
What problems it solves
  • Find ayat related to a topic (English or Arabic keyword).
  • See meaning-based and word-based connections instantly.
  • View related Qur’an ayat + Hadith side-by-side to learn context.
How to use (simple)
  1. Use only ONE input: English OR Arabic OR Ayah ID.
  2. Press Search (or hit Enter).
  3. Click an ayah in Search Results to load pairs.
Tip: Results are suggested links to help study. Always verify with trusted tafseer and scholars.
