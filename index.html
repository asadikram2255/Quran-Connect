Why this exists
Many people want to understand the Qur'an more deeply, but it's hard to connect related meanings across different ayahs and authentic ahadiths. This tool helps you explore those connections — by meaning and by shared root words — quickly, in one place.
What it does
  • Find ayat related to a topic in English or Arabic.
  • See meaning-based connections (semantic similarity).
  • See word-based connections (shared Arabic root words).
  • Dive deeper — click any related ayah to explore its own pairs.
How to use
  1. Choose a search type: English, Arabic, or Ayah ID.
  2. Type your query and press Search (or Enter).
  3. Click an ayah in results to see its related pairs.
  4. Click any related Quran ayah to keep exploring.
Results are AI-suggested links to aid study. Always verify with trusted tafseer and scholars.
